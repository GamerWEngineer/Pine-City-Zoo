MONKET TRAIL


Walk through the monkey habitat.

Located in the heart of the zoo, the Monkey Trail offers a unique opportunity to walk through a habitat designed for monkeys. You can observe them swinging from branches and interacting with each other in a naturalistic setting.

Experience the playful antics of monkeys up close. The Monkey Trail is a great place to learn about different species of monkeys and their behaviors.

Today's highlights:

  • Monkey Feeding - Watch the zookeepers feed the monkeys and learn about their diets.
  • Interactive Displays - Engage with displays that teach about monkey conservation.
  • Photo Opportunities - Capture memorable photos with the monkeys in the background.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The monkey trail is directly ahead from the entrance, as seen with a wood path and board.
