LOST FOREST


Experience nature in the forest.

Located in the heart of the zoo, the Lost Forest is a lush, green area that simulates a natural forest environment. You can walk through winding paths surrounded by trees and plants, and observe various animals that thrive in this habitat.

Explore the beauty of nature. The Lost Forest is a serene place to connect with nature and observe wildlife in a natural setting.

Today's highlights:

  • Birdwatching - Spot various bird species in their natural habitat.
  • Nature Trails - Enjoy a peaceful walk through the forest trails.
  • Wildlife Photography - Capture stunning photos of the flora and fauna.
  • Guided Tours - Join a guided tour to learn more about the ecosystem.
  • Interactive Exhibits - Engage with interactive displays about forest conservation.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The lost forest runs through the center of the zoo to the south-east part before the monket trail.
